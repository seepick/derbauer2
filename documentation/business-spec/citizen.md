# Citizen

* synonyms: population, people, peasants, villagers; workers
* yet another resource, "stored" in tents&co
* effects:
    * consume food per turn
        * starve (people die) if no food available
        * do a proper calculation: each citizen X food; (not just check if food > 0)
    * pay tax (gold income) per turn
        * actually the main (only?) income of gold
            * reproducing itself over time (depending on stats such as health/wealth/luck)
* used up (consumed) for army
    * one knight request X (1?) citizen
    * basically from worker to soldier
* they are affected by general state (happiness/health/wealth) of the city
    * e.g. higher happiness -> higher reproduction rate, more imigration (otherwise emigration)
    * e.g. lower health -> higher death rate, more likelihood of negative happenings (diseases)

## Ideas

* people act as worker, types e.g.: ingenieur, schmied, heiler
