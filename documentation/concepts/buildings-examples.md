# Building Examples

Areas:

* economical: affects resources (prod/cap)
* military: strengthen armies and increase cap
* social: increase happiness/karma
* feature enabler; e.g.: castle (for throne room)

## Economy/Resources

About:

* food cap/prod
* people cap/prod (reproduction rate, immigrants)
* gold prod
* trade benefits

Examples:

* House
    * +citizen cap
* Farm
    * +food prod
* Granary
    * +food cap, +rat protection
* "big granary":
    * resistince to happenings
* Marketplace
    * +gold prod (per people), -trade price modifier
* Mine
    * +gold prod, +people upcome (needs people to work)

## Military

About:

* unit cap/hire (=buy)
* enable hire (new types)

Examples:

* Barracks
    * +army cap, -army buy mod, +att/def mod
* Archery
    * +army cap, -army buy mod, +att/def mod
* Stable
    * +army cap, -army buy mod, +att/def mod
* Armoury
    * "improve stuff"?
* Wall
    * +defense
* "defence palisades"
    * less likely to get raided by bandits

## Research/Tech (upgrade/feature/action)

About:

* gain more research points; faster tech progression
* unlock certain features with it

Examples:

* School
* Library
* Alchemy

## Social

Examples:
